Our Services

Comprehensive design solutions tailored to your vision

Residential Design

Transform your home into a sanctuary of refined elegance. Our residential design services encompass complete home transformations, from initial concept development through final installation. We work closely with you to understand your lifestyle, preferences, and aspirations, creating spaces that are both beautiful and functional.

Our approach includes comprehensive space planning, custom furniture design, material selection, lighting design, and art curation. Whether you're renovating a single room or designing an entire estate, we ensure every detail reflects your unique vision while maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship and aesthetic excellence.

  • Complete home design & renovation
  • Custom furniture & millwork design
  • Space planning & architectural coordination
  • Material & finish selection
  • Lighting design & art curation
  • Project management & installation

Commercial Design

Consultation & Styling

For clients seeking expert guidance without a full-scale renovation, our consultation and styling services provide professional insights to refresh and refine your existing space. Perfect for homeowners looking to update their interiors with strategic changes.

Our consultation services include color palette development, furniture placement recommendations, art and accessory curation, and styling for photography or special events. We offer both in-person and virtual consultation options to accommodate your needs and schedule.

  • Design consultation & color planning
  • Furniture & accessory selection
  • Space styling & refresh services
  • Art curation & placement
  • Virtual design consultation
  • Design concept presentations
